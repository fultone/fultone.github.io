Here at Peak-A-Boo Toys we are all about fun!

When we opened in 2003 our goal was to provide a full service toy store to serve our locals and tourist alike.

We have quality toys from hundreds of different suppliers, from the big names like Lego and Mattel to smaller unique companies like Green Toys. We also have a large indoor play area that you can utilize on those cold winter days. Come on in and let the kids burn off some energy and try out some of our award winning toys.
